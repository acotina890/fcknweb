Is a line drawn outside the element's border, around elements, OUTSIDE the borders,
to make the element "stand out".

outline-style: required
    dotted dashed solid double groove ridge inset outset none hidden
outline-width: px, pt, cm, em,...,thin(1px), medium(3px), thick(5px)
outline-color: name, hex, rgb, hsl, invert (ensures that the outline is visible, regardless
    of color background)
outline-offset: adds space (transparent) between an outline and the edge/border
    px, pt, cm, em,...
